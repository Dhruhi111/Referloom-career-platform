# 📌 Overview

Referloom is an AI-powered career technology platform designed to transform how students connect with alumni, mentors, startups, and recruiters.

Traditional job portals often leave students competing among thousands of applicants with little guidance, networking opportunities, or referral support. Referloom addresses this challenge by creating a referral-first ecosystem where university alumni can mentor and refer students while AI assists users in identifying career opportunities that align with their skills, goals, and profile strength.

The platform combines career guidance, mentorship, AI-powered profile analysis, job matching, and referral networking into a single unified experience.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Millions of students graduate every year with limited industry exposure, weak professional networks, and little access to referrals.

At the same time:

* Alumni want to support their juniors but lack a structured platform.
* Startups struggle to discover qualified talent.
* Students often don't know how competitive their profiles are.
* Existing job portals focus on applications rather than relationships.

Referloom solves these problems by connecting all stakeholders through a collaborative and intelligent ecosystem.

---

# 💡 Solution

Referloom enables:

✅ Students to build stronger career profiles

✅ Alumni to mentor and refer students

✅ Recruiters to access curated talent pools

✅ Startups to hire through trusted referral networks

✅ AI-driven career guidance and job recommendations

The result is a more efficient, community-driven hiring process with better outcomes for students and employers alike.

# 🌟 Key Features

## 🤖 AI Fit Score

An intelligent scoring system that evaluates how closely a user's profile aligns with a job opportunity.

Factors may include:

* Skills
* Experience
* Education
* Certifications
* Career Goals
* Profile Completeness

Benefits:

* Faster job discovery
* Better application targeting
* Improved hiring efficiency

---

## 📈 AI Profile Strength Analyzer

Analyzes profile quality and generates a comprehensive score based on:

* Skills coverage
* Project portfolio
* Resume completeness
* Professional information
* Career readiness indicators

Users receive actionable recommendations for improvement.

Helping users become more competitive candidates.
